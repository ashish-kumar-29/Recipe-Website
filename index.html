Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Dough: Mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, ghee, and milk to form a soft dough.
  2. Syrup: Boil sugar, water, cardamom, and rose water to make syrup.
  3. Fry & Soak: Shape dough into balls, fry until golden, and soak in syrup.

Barfi

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Mix: Combine milk powder, sugar, and ghee in a pan. Cook on low heat.
  2. Set: Pour the mixture into a greased tray and spread it evenly. Let it cool and set
  3. Cut: Once set, cut the barfi into desired shapes and enjoy!

Gujiya

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Dry Fruits

Recipe

  1. Prepare the Dough: Mix all-purpose flour (maida) with ghee and knead it into a soft dough
  2. Prepare the Filling: In a pan, roast khoya (mawa) until it turns light brown.
  3. Roll out small portions of the dough,place the filling in the center and fry.

Motichoor Laddo

Ingredients

  • Besan (Gram Flour)
  • Sugar
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Prepare the Batter: Mix besan (gram flour) with water to form a smooth batter.
  2. Make the Sugar Syrup: Boil sugar and water to make a thick syrup.
  3. Combine and Shape: Mix the fried boondi with the sugar syrup.
